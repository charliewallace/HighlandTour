Charlie & Patty's family Album

Created 17 April 1999, to send to Wendy. These were picked from all the Wallace family photos I've got in digital form so far.

Suggestion: Wait a minute or so after opening this file, to allow the many photos below to load into memory. This will result in smoother scrolling. Also - the easiest way to scroll thru these is to use the page-up and page-down keys, and the up/down arrow keys. Holding down the arrow keys seems to work best.
